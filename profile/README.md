## 🧠 Overview

**Boris FX Continuum** is a comprehensive collection of professional visual effects, transitions, image restoration, compositing, and motion graphics plugins developed for modern video editing and post-production workflows.

The suite integrates directly with major editing and compositing applications, providing hundreds of GPU-accelerated effects, advanced transitions, cinematic filters, keying tools, color enhancement utilities, particle generators, and AI-powered processing modules. Continuum also incorporates Mocha planar tracking technology and Particle Illusion, allowing artists to combine sophisticated motion tracking with dynamic visual effects inside a unified production workflow. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

Designed for video editors, motion graphics artists, colorists, VFX professionals, broadcast studios, content creators, and Windows users.
